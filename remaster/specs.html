SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

Find out about the minimum specs and recommended system requirements you'll need
to play Marbles Playground Remastered on your favourite platform

MINIMUM SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

• OS: Windows 7 64bit or Linux
• Processor: AMD Athlon X2 or Intel Core 2 Quad
• Memory: 3 GB
• Graphics Card: AMD Radeon HD 5450 or equivalent
• Direct X: 10
• Drive Space: 1.5 GB

RECOMMENDED SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

• OS: Windows 10 64bit or Linux
• Processor: AMD Phenom X4 B95 or Intel Core i5 2400
• Memory: 6 GB
• Graphics Card: Nvidia GTX750ti or equivalent 
• Direct X: 11
• Drive Space: 2 GB
