ABOUT MARBLES PLAYGROUND

You have 1 chance only, No flashbacks, No another chance. If you fall you lose! Game of the hidden Easter Eggs, try to find all the secrets and the hidden Easter Eggs.

The game is featuring 2 marbles, The Red one and the Blue one, The Red marble is the game's hero, while the Blue one is the fast marble, it's faster than the Red marble by 5x times.

Marbles Playground contains 20 Levels , Every level may take up to 7 minutes to fully complete it with collecting the 5 stars and finding the level secret, So you may complete the game in 110 - 160 minutes depending on your gaming level.

What is Marbles Playground?

Play with marbles and try to reach the Finish Line. 
Marbles Playground is an Adventures Arcade game that you play as a marble in it and your goal is to reach the finish line to unlock the next level.

You have 1 chance only, No flashbacks, No another chance. If you fall you lose! Game of the hidden Easter Eggs, try to find all the secrets and the hidden Easter Eggs.

The game is featuring 2 marbles, The Red one and the Blue one, The Red marble is the game's hero, while the Blue one is the fast marble, it's faster than the Red marble by 5x times.

Marbles Playground contains 20 Levels , Every level may take up to 7 minutes to fully complete it with collecting the 5 stars and finding the level secret, So you may complete the game in 110 - 160 minutes depending on your gaming level.

The game is available for Android, and Windows PCs
